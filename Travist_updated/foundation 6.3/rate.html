Info

The Selling Rate represents the price banks sell the currency to you.

For instance, the selling rate of EUR / CHF is 1.0235 means that if you want to turn 100 Euro to Swiss Francs, the bank will offer you 102.35 Swiss Francs.

The Buying Rate represents the price banks buy the currency from you.

For instance, the buying rate of EUR / CHF is 1.0275 means that if you want to return the Swiss Francs to the bank and turn them back to Euros, then the bank will offer you 102.35 / 1.0275 = 99.61 Euro.
